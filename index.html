ROLLING SIR!

Good stories come in all lengths. You might find one best told in twenty minutes instead of one hundred and twenty minutes; a moment that speaks to your heart and cries out to be shared with the world. “The short film doesn’t supplant the feature, it nourishes it”:

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • done Only short film should be submitted and no documentaries. This entries can be short digitally.
  • done The film should be an original contribution of the participants Short Films should be submitted through Google drive and sent the link to this e-mail Id:rollingsirtechnokratz19@gmail.com The Short Film should be submitted on or before 20th February 2019
  • done DURATION: The film should not be more than 15 MIN including beginning and ending credits.
  • done The minimum length should be 30 SEC including credits.
  • done The short film can be in original language but should be submitted in ENGLISH.
  • done FORMAT: The film should be in watchable format,preferably in MPEG4/AVI format. The recommended ratio is 16:9.
  • done COPY RIGHT: The film must not contain any copy right work belonging to the third party unless the participant have the license to use it.
  • done The above mentioned instructions should be strictly followed else the short film team will be disqualified.
